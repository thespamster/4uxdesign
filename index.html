About 4uxdesign.com

My design pholiosophy is a simple one. As a fan of Occam's razor I believe that the simplest solution is very often the best one. Simple code is efficient code. This doesn't mean leaving features out but rather making sure that the features included are the ones that are needed.

Websites that are coded efficiently work better, have less bugs and will serve their purpose for longer. This will save you money and who doesn't want that?

Whether you are looking for a striking but simple landing page or a full e-commerce solution I can help you by building a site that is tailored to your needs. I build bespoke solutions at a fair price. I am a full stack developer which means I understand both the frontend (what you see in your browser) and the backend (the bit that stores the data, user information, product details, etc).
